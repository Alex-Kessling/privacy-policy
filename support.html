About Home Lovelace

Home Lovelace is a private family chore tracking app designed exclusively for the Kessling family. It helps busy families stay organized and keep track of daily tasks.

Getting Started

Home Lovelace allows your family to:

  • Track and assign chores to family members
  • Organize tasks by time of day (Morning, Midday, Night)
  • View completion status for each family member
  • Check local weather information
  • Set up repeating chores (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.)

Using the App

Adding a Chore

Tap the blue "+" button at the bottom right to create a new chore. You can:

  • Set a title and choose an emoji icon
  • Assign to one or more family members
  • Choose when it appears (Morning, Midday, Night, or all day)
  • Set it to repeat on a schedule

Completing Chores

Tap the circle checkbox next to a chore to mark it complete. Each time of day tracks completion separately, so a chore can be marked complete in the morning and will reset for the evening.

Permissions

Home Lovelace does not require any device permissions. The app:

  • Does NOT access: Calendar, Photos, Reminders, or Location
  • Only collects: Chore data that you manually enter
  • Weather: Shows weather for a hardcoded location (Havering, London) without accessing your device location
