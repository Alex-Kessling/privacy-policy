Overview

Home Lovelace is a private family chore tracking app designed exclusively for the Kessling family. We take your privacy seriously.

Information We Collect

The app collects and stores:

  • Chore data: Chore titles, assignments, completion status, and dates
  • Weather data: Your approximate location (Havering, London) to display local weather

How We Use Your Information

  • To display and track family chores
  • To show relevant weather information for your location
  • To sync chore data across family members' devices

Data Storage

All chore data is stored securely using Convex (convex.dev), a cloud database service. Weather data is retrieved from the free Open-Meteo API and is not stored.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties. This app is for private family use only.

Data Security

The app is protected by a family passcode. All data transmissions are encrypted.

Your Rights

As this is a private family app, you have full control over your data. You can:

  • Delete any chore data at any time
  • Request complete data deletion by contacting the app developer

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for family use and may be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond chore completion data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
